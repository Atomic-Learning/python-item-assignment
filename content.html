The items of a list are mutable, meaning you can change individual items using indexing:

Changing a Single Item

Use assignment to modify a list item at a specific index:

my_list = [1, 2, 3, 4] print(my_list) my_list[1] = 20 print(my_list) my_list[0] = 10 print(my_list)

Using Negative Indices

You can modify items using negative indices:

colors = ["red", "green", "blue"] colors[-1] = "yellow" print(colors)

Out of Bounds Assignment

You cannot assign to an index that doesn't exist:

my_list = [1, 2, 3] my_list[5] = 99
